Lambs International

What we do:

Lambs International works with communities to create income, empower the less fortunate, care for the Earth, and ultimately end world hunger and poverty.

We have all needed a helping hand in our lives. Start a cycle of positive change by helping others as you have been helped. The giving spirit grows exponentially with each person it impacts.

Join us in the fight against poverty! By becoming a Champion for the poor and raise funds for the poorest of the poor all over the world.
